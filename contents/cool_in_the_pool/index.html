What is this web page about?

In brazilian portuguese, or simply portuguese, there is the expression (eu tô) de boa na lagoa. Sometimes, while trying to express myself in english, I felt the need to use this particular expression. As far as I know, there is no english equivalent which preserves the rhymes, so I decided to create this webpage which contains my personal english translation of DBNL and a bunch of memes in a attempt to popularize this expression among my english fluents friends.

In this page you will find:

  • The meaning of de boa na lagoa and how it is used;
  • The translation;
  • Some memes with a dog floating in the pool;

The meaning of the expression.

According to Dicionário Informal, a portuguese analogue of the Urban Dictionary, DBNL intends to express well-being and relaxation. One could say Today I'm cool in the pool! or C in the P, bro!.

Fun facts.

The de boa na lagoa may also refer to:

  1. DBNL is the name of a hotel located in São Lourenço do Sul, in the state of Rio Grande do Sul.
  2. de boa na lagoa is the name of at least three songs on a popular song lyrics web site.

The translation.

The expression de boa na lagoa has a rhyme. For this reason alone, the chosen translation is Cool... In the Pool. I feel complete again.
